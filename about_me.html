About Me

I am currently a master's student in Computer Science at the University of Maryland and am working as a Software developer at Capital One. My undergraduate degree is from University of Maryland. I got a double degree in Computer Science and Astronomy. My interests are in artifical intelligence, machine learning, and space studies

I've been interested in Science since I was little. My interest in artifical intelligence and space started after I begain reading science fiction novels about robots and space travel. Specifically when I read Isaac Asimov's Galactic Empire series and Robots series. I was fascinated by this view of the future and wanted to be a part of it. I started at the University of Maryland as an aerospace engineering major. During my sophomore year, I took an introductory class for computer science in C++. I absolutely loved the class. I then realized that I belonged in computer science and I switched to a double major in Computer Science and Astronomy.

Since then I've done space research at NASA Goddard, developed a website at NASA Goddard, taught an astronomy class as a TA, and worked as a Software developer at Capital One. In my free time I do karate, play soccer, read science fiction, do photography, and play video games.
