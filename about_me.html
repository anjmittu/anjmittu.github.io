About Me

I am a recent graduate from the University of Maryland, College Park. I recevied a double degree in Computer Science and Astronomy.

I've been interested in Science since I was little. My interest in space started after I begain reading science fiction novels about space travel. Specifically when I read Isaac Asimov's Galactic Empire series and Robots series. I was fascinated by the future of space exploration and wanted to be a part of it.

I started at the University of Maryland as an aerospace engineering major. This is when I first became interested in Computer Science. As part of the aerospace engineering program, I had to take a comp sci class. I LOVED the class. It was then that I realized that my real interests lie not in the engineering of getting to space but in the study of space itself and using computer science to do it.
